Summary


Realizing that the oceans are full of plastic waste, the documentary filmmaker explores the environmental effects of pollution.

Audience Criticism


(2016) The documentary that journalist Craig Leeson started shooting after being influenced by the plastic waste he encountered in the ocean while searching for a blue whale.
Freediver Tanya Streeter accompanies Craig during the filming of the documentary. The documentary, which was shot in 20 locations around the world for 4 years, has terrifying images.
Tanya Streeter:
"Our record is 276 pieces of plastic from a 90-day old baby bird."
